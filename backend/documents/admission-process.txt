SED Academy - Admission Process

1. Application
   - Submit online application form
   - No application fee
   - Rolling admissions

2. Prerequisites
   - Basic computer literacy
   - Personal computer/laptop
   - Stable internet connection
   - 10+ hours per week for study

3. Technical Assessment
   - Basic programming test (for technical courses)
   - Logical reasoning test
   - No prior experience required for beginner courses

4. Interview
   - 30-minute video call
   - Discuss goals and expectations
   - Course fit assessment

5. Enrollment
   - Seat confirmation
   - Payment options:
     * Full payment (5% discount)
     * Installment plans available
     * Scholarships for eligible candidates

6. Onboarding
   - Welcome kit
   - Orientation session
   - Access to learning platform
   - Meet your mentor
